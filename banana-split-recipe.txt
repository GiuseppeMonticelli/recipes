Ingredients

- 2 bananas, halved lengthwise
- 1/2 pint vanilla ice cream
- 1 pint blackberries, picked over and washed
- 1 pint raspberries, picked over and washed
- Chocolate sauce, store bought or home made
- 1 cup peanuts

Directions

Place the bananas in a large dish. Scoop 3 to 4 balls of ice cream
onto the bananas. Top with the berries, chocolate sauce, and peanuts.
